ABOUT US

We are in need to STOP the GREED in our soup kitchens

We are here to introduce honesty in dealing with food received, cooked, and distributed to the people by Soup kitchens, we acheive this by upgrading the technology in organisations. An end to corruption in soup kitchens is our plan, we end corruption by designing a website, creating a database and an app, handing out electronic monitors in organisations. if you join us . we are sure we can.

We stop corruption to help buid a better society for all.



OUR GOAL

Help the less advantaged members of our communities to be fed regardless of their circumstances, Make sure that the corruption occurring inside community soup kitchens is terminated.



OUR VISION

Our vision is a world in which soup kitchens in our communities are free of corruption.
